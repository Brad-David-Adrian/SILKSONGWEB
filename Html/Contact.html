Silksong was chosen as the theme for this project beacuse ive been playin it recently and ive loved the game so far to the point im obsessed with it. the aesthetic of the game is just beautiful and the colors used in the game are just perfect for a dark theme with a touch of elegance. At the begining i wanted to make the page look more like the first zone of the game. A lush green with nature all artound. But after thinking it better i decided to imitate the loading screen of the game, with dark colors and red and amber details.
