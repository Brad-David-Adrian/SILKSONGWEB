Theme Justification

The Hollow Knight: Silksong theme was chosen to evoke the mystical and intricate world of Pharloom. The silk-weaving motif represents the interconnected threads of fate and the delicate balance of the game's ecosystem. This theme creates an immersive experience that draws visitors into the lore of the Weaver's Sanctum, where every element tells a story of craftsmanship, mystery, and the eternal dance of light and shadow.
